```ocaml
let foo y =
  let w = y + 3 in
  fun y -> (y + 3) + 2 + y * w
```

**Explanation:**

We substitute `x` with `y + 3` in the expression `let w = x in fun y -> x + 2 + y * w`.  This results in:

`let w = y + 3 in fun y -> (y + 3) + 2 + y * w`

The inlining of `x` happens in both the `w` definition and in the anonymous function.
